Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

We start with a site inspection to understand your space and vision. From there we develop a mood board, full costing, and move into execution. You're involved at every step.

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A single room typically takes 3–6 weeks. Full home transformations can range from 2–5 months. We always give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Do you work outside Lagos?

Our primary service area is Lagos, but we do take on select projects in Abuja and other cities. Reach out and we'll discuss what's possible for your location.

What's your minimum project budget?

We take on projects from ₦500,000 and above depending on the scope. Our team will always be transparent about costs before any commitment is made.

Can I buy drapes or flooring without a full design project?

Absolutely. You can order from our Drapes, Flooring, or Home Accessories ranges independently. Just reach out and our team will guide you.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We typically structure payments in milestones — an initial deposit, a mid-project payment, and a final balance on completion. Details are agreed upfront.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Eden Avenue Interiors truly offer?

We offer a complete interior design experience. one that goes beyond aesthetics. From concept to completion, we curate spaces that feel considered, functional, and effortlessly refined.

Do you handle full projects from start to finish?

Yes. We manage every layer of the process design direction, material sourcing, artisan coordination, and final styling. Ensuring a seamless, well-executed result.

Can I engage you for a specific part of my project?

Of course. While we specialise in full-service design, we also accommodate design consultations, material sourcing, or installation-only projects.

What is it like working with you?

Our process is structured yet flexible designed to give you clarity while allowing room for creativity. Every project begins with understanding your lifestyle, then translating it into a space that feels uniquely yours.

Do you provide visual concepts before execution?

Yes. We present curated mood boards, material palettes, and 3D visualisations so you can fully experience the direction before work begins.

Do you manage artisans and site supervision?

Yes. Execution is where design is either elevated or lost. we ensure it is meticulously handled, with close supervision and attention to detail at every stage.

How long does a project typically take?

Timelines vary depending on scope, but we prioritise efficiency without compromising finish. Each project is approached with a clear schedule from the outset.

Can you transform an already finished space?

Absolutely. We specialise in refining and elevating existing spaces, not just starting from scratch.

Do you source materials as part of your service?

Yes. We curate and supply materials that align with the overall design vision from SPC flooring and wall finishes to soft furnishings and final accents.

How do you ensure quality?

Every material we use is carefully selected for its durability, finish, and ability to age well within your space.

How is pricing structured?

Our pricing reflects the scope and level of detail required. This may include design fees, execution costs, and curated material selections.

What does payment look like?

Projects are structured in phases initial engagement, project progression, and completion thus allowing for clarity and accountability throughout.

Where do you operate?

Every material we use is carefully selected for its durability, finish, and ability to age well within your space.

How do you ensure quality?

We are based in Lagos and take on projects across Nigeria, depending on scope and requirements.

Do you handle sourcing and logistics?

Yes. We take full responsibility for procurement, coordination, and delivery, ensuring a smooth, stress-free experience for our clients.

What happens after the project is completed?

Our relationship doesn’t end at handover. We provide aftercare guidance and continued support to help you maintain the integrity of your space.

The Eden Avenue Interiors Standard

We take on projects where we can deliver something we're genuinely proud of. That means the right budget, the right timeline, and a client who trusts the process.

Every Eden Avenue project has one lead designer from the first site visit to the final handover. No rotating teams, no briefing gaps, no dropped details. Just one person who knows your space as well as you do.

We source locally first Lagos has exceptional craftsmen and suppliers that most studios overlook. We go international only when the quality demands it.

And we have never missed a handover date.

Our Visioneer

Oreoluwa Adesina

Oreoluwa Adesina brings a distinctive blend of structure, precision, and creativity into the world of interior design.

With a degree in Applied Mathematics from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology and a Diploma in Interior Architecture,

After five years at Guaranty Trust Bank as a Retail Banking Account Manager, she made a bold transition to pursue her passion for design, space planning, and creating refined living experiences.

Since founding Eden Avenue Interiors in 2019, she has worked with a diverse clientele, transforming residential and commercial spaces into elevated environments that embody both luxury and functionality.

From full-scale interior transformations to bespoke space planning and finishing solutions, Oreoluwa has built a reputation for delivering interiors that are thoughtful, detail-driven, and timeless.

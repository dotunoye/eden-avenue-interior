How We Work

From vision to reality

Every Eden Avenue project follows a careful, collaborative process designed to make sure your space turns out exactly the way you imagined it.

01

Site Inspection

We meet with you to understand your needs, preferences, budget and project goals. Our team conducts a thorough site inspection taking precise measurements, evaluating the layout and identifying every opportunity to maximise your space.

02

Mood Board & Design

Our designers create detailed concepts sketches, mood boards, floor plans and material recommendations all tailored to your vision. We present everything clearly, gather your feedback and refine until you're fully satisfied before moving forward.

03

Full Project Costing

Once the design is approved we develop the complete technical documentation 2D and 3D plans, interior elevations, lighting design, ceiling plans and full 3D renders so you can see exactly what your space will look like before a single nail is driven.

04

Project Execution

We bring everything to life procuring materials, mobilising skilled labour, managing the site and overseeing every detail from construction through to the final walkthrough. We only hand over when everything meets our standard and yours.

Custom Drapes & Textiles

Premium Fabrics for Every Room

Elevate Your Windows

Windows are the eyes of a room. Well-designed drapes not only frame your view but also control light, enhance privacy, and add texture and depth to your interior. We source premium fabrics from around the world and offer custom sizing, lining options, and motorized automation to suit any aesthetic and lifestyle.

01

Consultation & Selection

We bring fabric swatches to your home and discuss your light control, privacy, and style preferences.

02

Custom Measurement

Precise measurements ensure perfect fit and optimal functionality for your specific windows.

03

Sourcing & Tailoring

We procure your chosen fabrics and work with expert tailors for custom cuts and hems.

04

Professional Installation

Our team ensures flawless installation with hardware, linings, and automation where applicable.
